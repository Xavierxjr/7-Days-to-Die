This mod for 7 days to die expands the game arsenal with 7 new guns:
-Glock40 full auto pistol
-Mac10 submachine gun
-KSG12 shotgun
-M98 sniper rifle
-SCAR  assault rifle

----SPECIAL----
-M134 Minigun
-Flamethrower

All this guns use the already ingame types of ammo and mods.

I didn't make the graphic of the weapons neither sound or animation, i just put together pieces from old alphas 17-18 mods and already ingame existing things.
